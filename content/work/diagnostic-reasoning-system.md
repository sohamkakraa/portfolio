## Overview
I built a structured approach to narrowing root causes when evidence is incomplete.
The system helps teams list assumptions, rank constraints, and iterate toward the
most likely explanation without chasing noise.

## What I shipped
- A reasoning template that can be used in workshops or async reviews.
- A decision log format that captures why a path was chosen.
- A lightweight scoring model for competing hypotheses.

## Outcome
The process reduced rework and improved clarity around why a decision was made.
